A good question can be a wonderful thing. Well, I thought I might start a collection! To get better at questions, and to probe more deeply into my own skull, for both general psychic inquiries and more specific situations. If you have good ones, please share them with me! This list will be living. (Source/inspiration provided when known/suitable)

  • What important truth do very few people agree with you on? [Peter Thiel]

  • What are the key decisions you’ve faced in your life and what did you decide? Why did you go to X university? Why did you move to X city? [Peter Thiel]

  • Who is the most impressive person you know? What is most impressive about them?

  • What is your biggest current goal? What could you do in the next 15 minutes to get closer to it? (David Perell? I think?)

  • What is your favourite city? Why don’t you live there?

  • What do you imagine utopia to look like?

  • What are the important problems in your field, and why aren’t you working on them? [The Hamming question]

  • What important problems are you working on? If what you are doing is not important, and if you donâ€™t think it is going to lead to something important, why are you working on it? [The other Hamming question]

  • How much time do you spend with your parents every year, is that enough?

  • How much time do you spend with your kids every year, is that enough?

  • What secrets is nature not telling you? What secrets are people not telling you? [Peter Thiel]

  • Where is the frontier? Is that just the conventional wisdom of where it is? Where would you draw the frontier in this area? [Peter Thiel]

  • What is the most interesting mistake you’ve made?

  • What is the biggest decision you’ve made spontaneously?

  • If I commissioned you to write a biography about someone you dislike, who would you write about? Why?

  • Discovery comes from finding something completely new, or from uncovering an error/misconception in the current frame - where do you think is the most fruitful place to look for error in your field?

  • What part of yourself are you most afraid of?

  • Who are you avoiding messaging/talking to?

  • What are you avoiding doing?

  • Is this the most ambitious plan you could come up with? [Patrick Collison]

  • What would you propose if you had unlimited resources? [Patrick Collison]

  • If I gave you $ 1 000 000 and 2 years with absolutely no conditions, what would you work on?

  • What is the most wasteful and useless way to spend a day? What is the opposite of that?

  • When last were you proud of the way you spent a day?

  • What is the best year you have had? What’s stopping it from being this year?

  • What is the biggest thing you have ever asked for?

  • What is the biggest thing you have ever given away?

  • Is money important? (to you? In general?)

  • What book has most impacted your life?

  • What is the worst book you’ve ever read?

  • Why aren’t you being more ambitious?

  • What is an obvious thing to ask/do/make/work on that nobody seems to be asking/doing/making/working on?
