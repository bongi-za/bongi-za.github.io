Hello! I’m a biomechanist/sports scientist/researcher. I recently submitted my PhD in Sprint Biomechanics at the University of Pretoria, supervised by Helen Bayne and Neil Bezodis. Previously I have also spent time at UCAM CIARD and UCT ESSM (mostly in the MyoLab).

In former lives I’ve lived in Spain, Vietnam and Chile. I’m currently on the lookout for a new adventure - If you’re looking for a biomechanist or sport scientist with a broad interests, a love of data and writing, and a penchant for unusual places and opportunities, please get in touch!

Besides all that, you can probably find me being tall, running (slowly), or in a second-hand book store.

Contact: You can contact me via email or Twitter, I also respond well to carrier pigeon.
